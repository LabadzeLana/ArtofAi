About the Program

Where Human
Meets Machine

The ART of Using AI is an intensive educational program by GeoLab designed to bridge the gap between creative thinking and artificial intelligence. We believe AI is not a threat to human expression — it is its most powerful new medium.

Through lectures, live demonstrations, and collaborative workshops, participants gain practical, hands-on experience with the tools shaping our world — from generative models and language systems to AI-assisted design and decision-making.

The program is built for professionals, educators, designers, entrepreneurs, and anyone who wants to move from passive observer to active creator in the age of AI.
